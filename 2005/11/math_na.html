\(a_{n+1}=n+1+\dfrac{(n-1)!}{a_1a_2\cdots a_n} \;(n\geq 1)\).

\(a_1a_2\cdots a_n a_{n+1}\)
\(= a_1a_2\cdots a_n\times(n+1)+(n-1)!\)
